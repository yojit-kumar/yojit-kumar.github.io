I'm a 3rd year BSMS student majoring in Physics. My current focus is on learning how to translate physical systems into numerical models — from ODEs and eigenvalue problems to Monte Carlo simulations.

I'm working through the University of Oslo's FYS3150/4150 Computational Physics curriculum in C++, which covers linear algebra, differential equations, and statistical mechanics from scratch. I also work on ML and data science projects on the side.

I maintain two GitHub accounts: yojit-kumar for physics-oriented work and steven-scart for broader development projects.
